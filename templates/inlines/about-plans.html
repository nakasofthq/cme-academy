What is CME Academy?

Crypto Made Easy (CME) Academy is designed to be a teaching platform and a community full with crypto related resource that allows you and thousands of individuals in the Digital World of Cryptocurrency to connect, communicate, interact and learn with one another and become a better crypto trader. It also represents a world of creativity, our social network engages with users to push a more educated environment.

Cryptocurrency is a virtual form of currency that is based on Blockchain technology and is not controlled by any government. Examples of cryptocurrency include Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others, each with unique features and purposes within the digital financial landscape.

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency for secure, decentralised transactions, enabling swift online payments. It's used for investments and ICO fundraising and as a medium for diverse financial transactions, eliminating traditional banking intermediaries for seamless global transactions and investments.

This course covers comprehensive trading strategies and principles applicable to various trading durations, addressing both short-term trading, which involves taking advantage of price fluctuations within a short period, and long-term trading, which focuses on holding assets for an extended duration to benefit from potential market growth.

This course offers enriched market insights, skill enhancement in trading strategies and risk management, and proficient knowledge of market trends. It equips individuals with practical skills for informed and effective cryptocurrency trading.

Upon course completion, delegates will receive a certificate validating the skills gained through this course.
