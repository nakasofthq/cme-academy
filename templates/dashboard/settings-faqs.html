What is Cryptocurrency?
Cryptocurrency is a virtual form of currency that is based on Blockchain technology and is not controlled by any government. Examples of cryptocurrency include Bitcoin, Ethereum, and many others, each with unique features and purposes within the digital financial landscape.

What is the uses of Cryptocurrency?
Cryptocurrency is a digital currency for secure, decentralised transactions, enabling swift online payments. It's used for investments and ICO fundraising and as a medium for diverse financial transactions, eliminating traditional banking intermediaries for seamless global transactions and investments.
